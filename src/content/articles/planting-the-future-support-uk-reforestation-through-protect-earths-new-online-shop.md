At a time when the climate crisis and biodiversity loss are demanding urgent action, Protect Earth is stepping up with an exciting new way for you to make a direct difference—[our online shop](https://shop.protect.earth/). Whether you're passionate about supporting greener spaces, improving local ecosystems, or simply looking for a meaningful gift, there's now a simple way to get involved.

## What Can You Buy?

![$2](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/66f54aab97a2451c86736b2d/1727351468149-HU48XYH46ADWITOKPWLP/Tree+shop.png?format=original)

Protect Earth's shop offers native, broadleaf trees and hedges—species that belong in the UK, thriving in our climate and soil. These plants do more than grow. They help regenerate soil, create habitats for wildlife, conserve water, and purify the air, underpinning healthy ecosystems that are vital for future generations.

You can purchase individual trees or opt for bundles, allowing you to make a more significant impact. A monthly subscription option is available if you want to make an ongoing contribution.

Your purchases fund the planting of new saplings across the UK, ensuring a steady stream of greenery contributing to our natural landscapes.

## Where Does Your Money Go?

Each tree or hedgerow you purchase will be planted at one of Protect Earth's projects - designed to create long-term habitats that benefit wildlife and contribute to carbon sequestration.

After your saplings are planted, you'll receive a certificate with details on where and when they were planted.

It's a meaningful way to stay connected to your contribution and see your impact on the environment.

## Trees as Gifts or Legacies

![$2](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/66f54aab97a2451c86736b2d/1727351468155-6YZYYTHOJ44QR5O162GI/image.png?format=original)

Trees are more than just plants—they’re living legacies. Some trees, like oaks and yews, can live for over 1,000 years. Imagine how much impact they can have.

Buying a tree is a powerful way to celebrate someone’s life, achievements, or important milestones. It’s a way to leave a lasting, positive mark on the world, whether honouring a loved one, celebrating a personal accomplishment, or simply supporting the planet.

You can even make buying trees or subscribing to plant regularly a personal achievement—perhaps your goal is to plant 100 trees. Businesses, too, can get involved by creating natural habitats through ongoing tree-planting subscriptions, incorporating their environmental support into their messaging to employees and customers.

## Why an Online Shop?

We generally rely on partnerships and grants to fund our projects, but those funds alone aren't enough with the growing urgency to restore biodiversity and combat climate change.

We invite individuals to join us in accelerating change by opening this shop. The climate crisis isn't waiting, and neither are we.

With your purchases, we can plant more trees, create more habitats, and build a healthier future. [Visit the shop today](https://shop.protect.earth/) and start planting the seeds of tomorrow's natural world.
