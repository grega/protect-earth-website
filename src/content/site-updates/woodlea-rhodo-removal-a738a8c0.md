Our second visit was by Phil, who, along with his machete and loppers, cleared out some of the rhododendrons that were encroaching on the ground. These plants, while superficially pretty, are actually quite the pain in our rears. They’re just too good at being plants, and thus will outcompete pretty much every other plant in the area for resources. We don’t want a monoculture, which is what the rhododendrons want! So, out went Phil with his tools!
